I make sure teams build the right thing.

I'm a Business Analyst working at the seam between business and engineering. I run discovery, turn ambiguous goals into precise BRDs, user stories and UAT, and own the backlog as Product Owner — then I measure what actually ships.

The results matter more than the documents:

~30%less rework
26.54%lower churn
10,000+records analysed
20+projects delivered

What I bring

  • Requirements with no loose ends — interviews, workshops and JAD turned into BRD/FSD and user stories with airtight acceptance criteria.
  • Delivery ownership — Agile/Scrum, backlog grooming, UAT and stakeholder sign-off, all the way to a clean release.
  • Proof in data — Power BI, SQL and Excel to validate decisions and show impact, not just opinions.
  • Alignment across teams — UML and process maps that get business and engineering reading from the same page.
Currently open to Business Analyst roles where I can own outcomes end to end — ideally with a team building at scale.
